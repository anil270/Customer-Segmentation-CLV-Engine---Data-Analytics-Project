#  WEEK 3: VISUALIZATION & BUSINESS INSIGHTS

## Overview
Week 3 focused on the final presentation layer. We converted raw numbers into high-impact visuals to help business leaders make decisions instantly.

## Technical Execution
- **Presentation**: `presentation.html` (Interactive Executive Deck)
- **Dashboard**: `powerbi/Consumer360_Dashboard.pbix`

---

##  Executive Insights (Presentation)
Our interactive presentation highlights the "Big Picture" metrics:
- **Total Revenue**: ~£16M+ generated from global sales.
- **Top Product**: "Regency Cakestand 3 Tier" (£273K revenue).
- **Market Reach**: UK dominates (82%), but the Netherlands has the highest value per customer.
- **Seasonality**: Peak sales detected every November due to holiday demand.

---

##  Power BI Dashboard (Deep-Dive)
We built a multi-page dashboard for granular analysis:
1. **Overview Page**: Real-time KPI tiles (Revenue, Customer Count, Orders).
2. **RFM Analysis Page**: Interactive scatter plots to filter customers by segments like "Champions" or "At-Risk".
3. **Market Basket Page**: Shows which products should be sold as bundles.
4. **Geographic Page**: A global map showing where the most profitable customers are located.

---

##  Data-Driven Strategy
Instead of guessing, the business can now:
- **Target specific segments**: Send discount codes only to "About to Sleep" customers.
- **Optimize Inventory**: Stock up on products that are frequently bought together.
- **Global Expansion**: Focus marketing efforts on high-value countries like the Netherlands and Germany.
