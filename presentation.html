Key Business Recommendations

⚠ Geographic Concentration Risk

82.7% revenue from UK alone. A single market disruption could severely impact business. Prioritise EIRE, Germany and France for growth campaigns.

High Priority

💡 Q4 Seasonal Spike

Revenue jumps 65-90% in Oct–Nov every year. Pre-load inventory and run targeted campaigns in September to capture early holiday shoppers.

Action: Sep Campaign

📈 Netherlands High-Value Segment

23 Netherlands customers average £23,320 each — 9.5× the UK average. Launch VIP programme targeting B2B wholesale buyers in NL.

Revenue Opportunity

📅 Thursday is Peak Day

Thursday drives £3.46M (21.7% of revenue) with 156K orders. Schedule email campaigns and promotions on Tuesday–Wednesday for Thursday conversion.

Marketing Timing

🏆 Top 3 Products = £680K

Regency Cakestand, White Heart T-Light, and Jumbo Bag together drive £680K. Ensure these are always in stock and cross-promoted.

Stock Priority

⚡ Weekend Revenue Gap

Saturday generates only £9,803 — nearly zero. The business is effectively closed on weekends. Online self-service ordering could capture lost weekend demand.
